## How to Report Issues

### Quick Report (For Simple Bugs)
Send us:
1. **What you were trying to do**
2. **What happened instead**
3. **Screenshot (if possible)**

### Detailed Report (For Complex Issues)
Include:
1. **Steps to reproduce:**
   - I clicked...
   - Then I...
   - Then this happened...
2. **Expected behavior:** What should have happened
3. **Actual behavior:** What actually happened
4. **Browser & Device:** e.g. "Chrome on Mac" or "Safari on iPhone"
5. **Screenshot or video** (highly appreciated!)

## Tips for Effective Testing

1. **Try to break things** - Don't be gentle! Click rapidly, upload huge files, enter weird characters.

2. **Test edge cases:**
   - Very long titles/descriptions
   - Special characters in names
   - Empty forms
   - Multiple tabs open

3. **Think like a new user** - Is it intuitive? Would a first-time user understand?

4. **Test on mobile** - If possible, try on your phone/tablet

5. **Check different browsers** - Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge
